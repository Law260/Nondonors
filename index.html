🔍 How to use The Investigation

🤖 Made with AI — ChatGPT to research and summarise published reports; Claude Code to build the app. There may be mistakes. This is an exploration of what AI tools can create.

This section lets you explore why 50 people from a typical UK audience don’t give to charity. Use it to understand the barriers before planning your response.

The grid

All 100 people are shown at once. Green faces already give. The 50 non-donors don’t — click any of them to explore their story. The colours show which segment or barrier type they belong to (use “Colour by” to change).

Segments

The 50 non-donors fall into 7 distinct audience segments. Selecting a segment highlights those people in the grid so you can see who they are.

Barriers

11 evidence-backed barriers explain what stops people giving. Each is grounded in real research. Selecting a barrier highlights the people most affected.

🤖 The barrier data was synthesised by ChatGPT from 52 published reports, then reviewed and structured for this tool. Individual figures may not match primary sources exactly.
